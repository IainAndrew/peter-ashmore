Peter

Ashmore

Audio Engineer

Peter Ashmore is a recording/mixing engineer and producer, having worked with many artists of all types, from folk, to pop punk, to electronic. Currently working as house engineer in the unique Orphan Recording Studios in South Dublin's Inchicore, which boasts of being 'an Aladdin's Cave of rare and vintage recording equipment, instruments and amplifiers".

Peter also uses his home studio Ghost Studio in North Dublin's Rush Village for further overdubbing, editing and mixing.
