Peter Ashmore Audio Engineer

I’m Peter Ashmore, a recording/mixing engineer and producer.

About Me

I’ve been recording music since I was twelve years old and have been doing so professionally for the past six years. Since then I’ve worked with a lot of different artists in all kinds of locations from the greatest studios in Ireland to your average living room. In those years I trained with some of the best recording, mixing and mastering engineers in the country.

Orphan Recording Studios, Inchicore
